# Onboarding Organizxation

Alexa Accuardi

The goal of this initiative is to improve the process for organizing information related to active enrollments within HAAG. The deliverable will be a procedure that outlines the process for getting organized at the start of each semester.

## Initiative Framework

### 1. Discovery

The first step of this process will be to document and learn about current processes in place.

- The process of documenting a new team (or changes to an existing team)
- How HAAG keeps track of active, upcoming, and past research groups
- How we track operational action items related to onboarding
- Interview admins, TAs, researchers, and managers to understand what is working and what areas could be improved related to organization
- What budget does HAAG have for software and other tooling
- What existing tooling does HAAG/GT have access to

### 2. Hypothesis

My hypothesis is that adopting a tool such as Notion will allow for better organization and greater clarity of HAAG admin processes.

### 3. Experiment

To experiment with this, I will use the information gathered during the discover phase to implement organization within Notion. If Notion is not within the program's budget, this phase will involve researching other tools/software available to us.

### 4. Report & Iterate

With the new method in place, I plan to interview stakeholders and understand how the new system either improves or fails to improve upon the current processes. The primary artifact of this initiative will be a procedure that documents the new process.
